# 📘 Software Engineering - Verification, Validation & Quality

---

# 🔹 Verification & Validation

## ✅ Verification
👉 **"Are we building the product right?"**

- Ensures the software follows the **specifications**
- Checks if code and design match requirements

📌 Example:  
If the requirement is to build a program that adds two numbers  
→ Verification ensures correct implementation

---

## ✅ Validation
👉 **"Are we building the right product?"**

- Ensures the software meets **user needs**

📌 Example:  
User needs a full calculator  
But only addition is implemented  
→ Validation fails ❌

---

## 🔹 Key Differences

| Verification | Validation |
|-------------|-----------|
| Building the product right | Building the right product |
| Focus on design & code | Focus on user needs |
| Before execution | After execution |

---

## 🔹 V&V Process

- Continuous across all development stages

### 🎯 Objectives:
- Detect defects  
- Ensure usability  

⚠️ Software must be **good enough**, not perfect

---

## 🔹 Types of Verification

### Static Verification
- Code review  
- Design review  
- Inspections  

### Dynamic Verification
- Testing  

---

## 🔹 Software Testing

> Shows presence of errors, not their absence

### Types:
- **Defect Testing** → finds bugs  
- **Validation Testing** → checks requirements  

---

## 🔹 Testing vs Debugging

| Testing | Debugging |
|--------|----------|
| Finds errors | Fixes errors |

---

## 🔹 Software Inspections

- No execution required  
- Done early  
- Finds defects effectively  

---

## 🔹 V&V Planning

- Start early  
- Save time  
- Reduce errors  

---

# 🔹 Software Quality Concepts

## ✅ What is Quality?

Quality refers to **characteristics or attributes of a product**

### In Software:
- **Quality of Design** → requirements & design  
- **Quality of Conformance** → implementation  
- **User Satisfaction** → quality + delivery + budget  

---

## 🔹 Views of Quality

- **User View** → meets user goals  
- **Product View** → features & functions  
- **Value-Based View** → worth the cost  

---

## 🔹 Software Quality Definition

> A software process that produces a **useful product with measurable value**

---

## 🔹 Effective Software Process

- Provides structure to build quality software  
- Includes:
  - Project management  
  - Engineering practices  
  - Reviews & testing  

---

## 🔹 Useful Product

A high-quality product:
- Meets requirements  
- Works reliably  
- Is error-free (as much as possible)  

---

## 🔹 Adding Value

### For Organization:
- Less maintenance  
- Fewer bugs  
- Lower support cost  

### For Users:
- Better functionality  
- Faster processes  

---

## 🔹 McCall’s Quality Factors

### Product Operation:
- Correctness  
- Reliability  
- Usability  
- Efficiency  
- Integrity  

### Product Transition:
- Portability  
- Reusability  
- Interoperability  

### Product Revision:
- Maintainability  
- Flexibility  
- Testability  

---

## 🔹 ISO 25010 Quality Model

### Quality in Use:
- Effectiveness  
- Efficiency  
- Satisfaction  
- Freedom from risk  
- Context coverage  

### Product Quality:
- Functional suitability  
- Performance efficiency  
- Compatibility  
- Usability  
- Reliability  
- Security  
- Maintainability  
- Portability  

---

## 🔹 Software Quality Dilemma

- Low quality → no users  
- Perfect quality → too expensive & slow  

👉 Solution: **Balance (Good Enough Software)**

---

## 🔹 Cost of Quality

- **Prevention Costs** → planning, training  
- **Appraisal Costs** → testing, reviews  
- **Internal Failures** → fixing bugs  
- **External Failures** → customer complaints  

---

## 🔹 Risk & Quality

Low quality software:
- Increases security risks  
- Causes failures  
- Leads to legal issues  

---

## 🔹 Achieving Software Quality

### Requires:
- Good management  
- Strong engineering practices  

### Includes:
- Quality Control → testing & inspections  
- Quality Assurance → monitoring & reporting  

---

# 🔥 Final Summary

- Verification → build the product right  
- Validation → build the right product  
- Quality → deliver value + reliability  

✔️ Use:
- Testing  
- Inspections  
- Planning  
→ To ensure high-quality software
